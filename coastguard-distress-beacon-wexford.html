Search Launched After Distress Beacon Detected Off Wexford Coast

Off the Wexford Coast · 2025-11-01 · Coast Guard

A search operation was launched after an Emergency Position Indicating Radio Beacon (EPIRB) signal was detected off the Wexford coast on Saturday 1 November.

RNLI lifeboats from several stations were launched and a fixed-wing aircraft provided by the Air Corps conducted an aerial search of the indicated area.

Despite an extensive search over a wide area, no vessel or debris was located. The signal ceased after a period of time, and further analysis is being carried out to establish the exact source.

The Coast Guard has stated that all alerts of this nature are treated as genuine until a satisfactory explanation is found.

Mariners are reminded to ensure that safety equipment, including EPIRBs and personal locator beacons, is correctly registered and maintained.

Further information
