Public Advised of Planned Test of National Phone Alert System

Ireland · 2025-11-11 · Emergency Alerts

Authorities have reminded the public of a planned test of the national emergency alert system, which uses mobile phone networks to deliver urgent messages.

During the test window, compatible devices may receive a short notification accompanied by a distinct sound.

Officials stress that no action will be required from the public and that the test is part of ongoing efforts to enhance emergency communication.

Further information

  • Details may be updated as additional information is confirmed by official sources.
  • Members of the public with relevant information are encouraged to contact the appropriate authorities.
  • For emergencies, always dial 999 or 112.
