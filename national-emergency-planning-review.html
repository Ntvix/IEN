Government Announces Review of National Emergency Planning Framework

Dublin · 2025-11-06 · National

The Government has announced a review of the national framework governing emergency planning and response across the State. The review will look at coordination between emergency services, local authorities and national agencies.

Officials said the process will include engagement with frontline responders, community groups and voluntary organisations involved in emergency support.

An interim report is expected next year, with any recommended changes to be brought before the Oireachtas.
