EU Civil Protection Mechanism Activated Following Severe Flooding

Europe · 2025-11-04 · International

Severe flooding has prompted a number of EU member states to request assistance through the EU Civil Protection Mechanism.

Irish officials say there are currently no requests for personnel from Ireland, but the situation continues to be monitored.

The Department of Foreign Affairs has advised Irish citizens in affected regions to follow local guidance and register their details where appropriate.

Further information

  • Details may be updated as additional information is confirmed by official sources.
  • Members of the public with relevant information are encouraged to contact the appropriate authorities.
  • For emergencies, always dial 999 or 112.
