Yellow Rain Warning Issued for Western Counties

West of Ireland · 2025-11-08 · Weather

Met Éireann has issued a yellow rain warning for parts of the west of Ireland, citing the potential for heavy downpours and localised spot flooding.

Motorists are being advised to allow extra time for journeys and to be mindful of surface water.

Local authorities say drainage crews are on standby in case of any issues arising overnight.

Further information

  • Details may be updated as additional information is confirmed by official sources.
  • Members of the public with relevant information are encouraged to contact the appropriate authorities.
  • For emergencies, always dial 999 or 112.
